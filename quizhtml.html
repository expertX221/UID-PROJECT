FAQ

How does the quiz work?
Select a category and answer questions.
Is timer mandatory?
Timer can be added later.
How is the score calculated?
Each correct answer gives you 1 point. No negative marking for wrong answers.
Does my score get saved?
Your latest score is added to the leaderboard after completing a quiz.
What happens if I quit the quiz midway?
Your progress will be lost and the quiz will not be counted in the leaderboard.
Is there a time limit for answering questions?
Currently, there is no time limit. A timed quiz mode may be added in future updates.
Why don’t I see my name on the leaderboard?
Only top players are shown on the leaderboard. Try scoring higher to appear on it!
Can I play on mobile devices?
Yes, QuizHub is responsive and works on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
